My Top Tea Shops

  • Mei Leaf exists to bring true tea culture to London and beyond. Their hand-sourced teas run the full range of tea types: White, Green, Matcha, Yellow, Oolong, Black, Raw and Ripened Puerh. They run a physical store and teahouse in Camden and have an extensive website with excellent resources on all things tea culture.
  • Postcard Teas specialises in sourcing teas from small scale producers who farm less than 15 acres. Based in Mayfair, they operate a physical storefront and online shop, offering a well-curated selection of teas from China, Japan, Korea, Vietnam, Taiwan, and India.
